Why Use Circuit Drafter?

cloud_off

100% Client-Side

Runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to a server.

construction

Intuitive Tools

A simple toolbar with essential components, smart wiring that snaps to a grid, and automatic junction dots.

keyboard

Keyboard Shortcuts

Speed up your workflow with intuitive hotkeys for tools (S, W, T), editing (Ctrl+Z), and component manipulation (R, Del).

image

High-Res Export

Export your finished schematic as a high-quality PNG image, perfect for documentation, reports, or sharing.

code

Open & Free

Built with vanilla JavaScript and zero dependencies. The code is open-source and free to use, modify, and learn from.

bolt

Instant Access

No downloads, no lengthy setup. Just open the page and start drawing your circuit immediately.
